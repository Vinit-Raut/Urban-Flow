The Urban Flooding Challenge: A Growing Concern

Urban flooding is not just a seasonal inconvenience; it poses a significant threat to our cities and communities. As climate change intensifies, the frequency and severity of flooding events are increasing, leading to dire consequences.

  • Increased property damage costs, leading to financial strain on families and businesses.
  • Threats to public health due to water contamination and disease outbreaks.
  • Disruption of daily life and economic activities, affecting livelihoods.
  • Strain on emergency services and urban infrastructure, making recovery efforts more challenging.
